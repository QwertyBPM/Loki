Panetela

The Panetela is a Cuban dessert

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 2 cups of white sugar
  • ½ Cup of milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 1½ baking power
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla
  • 1 pinch of salt

Instructions

    Before we begin it is essential for the eggs to be at room temperature. Make sure to seperate the clears from the yolk, to prevent a bad egg contaminating the rest.

  1. First, we will begin with our wet ingrediants, add the yolks with half of our sugar. Mix for 1 minute, and slowly add the milk to the mix and stir swiftly for 3 minutes.
  2. Now we combine our dry ingrediants, Flour, salt, and baking powder. Gently add the dry ingredients to our wet inggrediants to avoid lumps in the batter.
  3. Stir slowly for 2 minutes till we get an even batter.
  4. Beat the egg whites with the rest of our remaining sugar at high velocity. Then add the vanilla
  5. Fold in the egg whites with the rest egg yolk mixture till fully mixed.
  6. Preheat the oven to 350°F or 180°C.
  7. Bake for 40 or 45 minutes depending on the mold you are using. Avoid opening the open to prevent uneven cooking.
  8. Once the timer is up you make enjoy your panetela or add coconut shavings, jam, or syrup for extra enjoyment. Enjoy.
